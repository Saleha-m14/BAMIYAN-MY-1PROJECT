Must see places in Bamiyan

Boda Statics


The Buddha statues are 1600 years old and are known as Salsal and Shamameh which are one of the masterpieces of Buddhism. The height of Salsal is 53 meters and the height of Shamameh is 35 meters. Both idols are carved in the hearth of mountain and are located on the silk road, approximately 3 Kilometers north of Bamiyan. These two statues have been registered as wonders of the world by UNESCO. These idols were destroyed by Taliban in 2001.

Band-e Amir


Besides these two statues, Band-e Amir is also a tourist attraction, which is located in Bamiyan province of Afghanistan. Since 2009, it has been named as the first national park in Afghanistan by the government. Koprak is Band-e Amir's fountin that locates 9 miles away from it. Band-e Amir is a set of sex lakes(Band-e Zolficar, Gholaman, Qanabr, Panir, Pudina and Haibat). The largest lake in this area is Band Zulfiqar which surrounds 490 hectares of land.
